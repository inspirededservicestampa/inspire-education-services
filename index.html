Practical, Evidence-Based Parent Training

We help Pasco County families transform defiance into cooperation using strategies you can apply the same day—rooted in Parent–Child Interaction Therapy (PCIT), The Incredible Years®, and Collaborative & Proactive Solutions (CPS).

Programs

  • Effective commands & reinforcement systems
  • De-escalation, routines, & CPS problem-solving

PCIT-Style Parent Coaching (Ages 3–8)

  • Weekly 60-minute live coaching
  • CDI → PDI phases
  • In-person or telehealth

Bootcamps & Minis

  • Defiance → Cooperation (3 hours)
  • Bedtime Battles (2 sessions)
  • Fast, practical wins
